Andrés Bonifacio y de Castro

The Father of Philippine Revolution

Here's a Timeline in Andrés Bonifacio y de Castro Life

  • 1863 - Born Tondo, Manila. Parents are Santiago Bonifacio and Catalina De Castro
  • 1892 - Became a founding member of La Liga Filipina, with Jose Rizal. It is an organization aiming for political reforms in Spain's colonial government of the Philippines
  • 1892 - Founded the Kataastaasang Kagalanggalangang Katipunan ng mga Anak ng Bayan or Katipunan. It aimed to gain independence from Spain through an armed revolt.
  • 1893 or 1894 - Married his second wife, Gregoria de Jesus.
  • 1896 - Started the uprising with the Cry of Pugad Lawin or Cry of Balintawak. 1897 - Ordered to be arrested by Emilio Aguinaldo, the then-President of the First Philippine Republic.
  • 1897 - Executed at age 33, with his brother Procopio, in the mountains of Maragondon.
